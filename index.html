Our Mission
Unikbase's mission is to give a digital existence to physical objects that have value in the eyes of collectors.

The digital twin of collectibles, vintage, and art works, offers 3 benefits: security, fluidity, value
Digital identity
It is the digital identity card of your collectible, which reinforces their security. It creates trust, in a market that needs it: authentication, certificates of ownership, photos, etc.
Support
It is the support that facilitates services related to your collectibles, for enhanced fluidity: insurance, transfer, marketing, etc.
Verified
It is a verified digital twin which can be used & exposed digitally (exhibitions, virtual gallery, metaverse)
The Solution
The digital twin contains elements of different natures: Reference documents relating to the object (acquisition slip, expertise, etc.) Photos, 3D scan, description Events related to the life of the object: transactions, restorations, repairs, etc.

The documents contained in the digital twin are assigned with certainty to the physical object, which establishes an additional proof of ownership, essential in case of sale or other event in the life of your object.The data contained in the digital twin is secured thanks to the combination of two technologies: An indelible, unalterable, and invisible marking technology recognized and used by the police & judicial authorities
A digital token hosted on a private blockchain compatible with Ethereum. Blockchain technology guarantees the inalterability of data, i.e. the ability to verify that the information in a given token has been preserved in its integrity, and the date on which it was added. However, the owner of the token remains in control of the nature of the data that is kept in the tokens. At any time it can delete information. There will then be nothing left, except the trace that information has been deleted.
